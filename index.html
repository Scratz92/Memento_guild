    About Us

    We are a casual raiding guild on Alliance Auchindoun. Guild was formed during Legion and consist of a tight core of different IRL as well as online friends. We raid 9 hours per week over three raid days: Monday, Wednesday and Sunday from 20:00 to 21:00 GMT +1.

    Our goal as a guild is to clear content on highest difficulty before next set of raid tier is released.

    Even if we say we are a casual guild we don’t consider our performance from our raiders to be casual at all. We see ourselves as a place for raiders with hardcore performance, but an unwillingness to raid everyday more than 3 days a week.

    We believe that wipes should be caused by a flaw of tactics rather than by personal mistakes Even if we keep a relaxed environment and we goof around a lot on voice communication, we expect that everyone can bring full focus when it comes to bosses.

    Outside of raids we expect our raiders to at least do a Mythic 15+ for the weekly chest as well as that our raiders that don’t have the 54 trait yet to actively try and achieve it.

    Guild Rules

    • All BoE'e from guild raids goes to the guild bank
    • A trials period will never last more than 3 weeks
    • Never PUG what the guild is currently raiding, unless you're unable to attend
